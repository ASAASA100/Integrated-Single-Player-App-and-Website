Case Study

The Compliance Paradox.

Finance

The Challenge

The client, a $2B AUM Wealth Management firm, was bleeding younger clients to Robo-Advisors due to slow onboarding speeds. However, the Compliance Director refused to adopt generic AI tools due to fear of data leakage.

The Solution: "Governed AI"

Unlike generalist agencies, we didn't just install a chatbot. We built a secure pipeline.

  • Step 1 (The Audit): We mapped exactly where client data lived and defined "Red Lines."
  • Step 2 (The Architecture): We selected an Enterprise LLM wrapper with Zero Data Retention.
  • Step 3 (Human-in-the-Loop): We automated drafting, but required advisor approval for finalization.

The Result

Six months later, the firm saved $120,000/year in admin hours, reduced onboarding to 4 hours, and passed a third-party security audit with zero critical findings.

Case Study

Secure Patient Data.

Healthcare

The Challenge

A mid-sized hospital network struggled with unstructured patient intake forms, leading to 30% coding errors and billing delays.

The Solution: Private NLP

We deployed a HIPAA-compliant NLP model to extract and code symptoms automatically.

  • Step 1 (Data Isolation): We deployed an on-premise model ensuring no PII left the facility.
  • Step 2 (Training): Tuned the model on anonymized historical records.
  • Step 3 (Clinician Review): Output codes were flagged for rapid doctor verification.

The Result

Billing accuracy improved to 98%. Administrative overhead reduced by 30%. Clinicians saved 2 hours per day on paperwork.

The Network Automator.

Telecoms

The Challenge

A regional telecom provider faced high churn due to slow reaction times during network outages. Manual triage took 45 minutes on average.

The Solution: CPMAI Prediction

We implemented a Predictive ML model using CPMAI methodology to analyze network logs in real-time.

  • Step 1 (Aggregation): Unified log streams from 5,000 nodes.
  • Step 2 (Prediction): ML model identifies anomalies 10 minutes before failure.
  • Step 3 (Routing): Automated tickets sent to field teams instantly.

The Result

Triage time reduced to seconds. Network uptime improved to 99.9%. Customer satisfaction scores rose by 15 points.
